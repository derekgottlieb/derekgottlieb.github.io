New Dell 20" LCD

November 4, 2004
tech

So apparently Dell is now shipping a 20" widescreen LCD ala the Apple Cinema dispay. Looks like it’s got pretty sweet specs in addition to being priced several hundred less than Apple’s. Now if only Dell would have some outrageous deal on it. :)

  • 1680x1050 resolution
  • 16:10 aspect ratio
  • 16ms refresh (12ms gray-to-gray)
  • 600:1 contrast ratio
  • 300cd/m2 brightness
  • analog (VGA) and digital (DVI) inputs, as well as video inputs for S-video and composite connections
  • Picture-in-Picture and picture-by-picture support
  • four USB2.0 connectors
  • rotates into a portrait display position
